Magic Box Puzzle

Magic Box Puzzle includes jigsaw puzzles, hidden object, sequences and memory match games. Thousands of new games in one app with regular updates!

iOS: https://itunes.apple.com/app/id1448598576

Android: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.digectsoft.magicboxpuzzle


Now all the best puzzles in one app. You become a real detective with hidden object game. Just choose the category you interested in and be fascinated with a mysterious atmosphere. Jigsaw puzzles is not only beautiful pictures, but they may help you to develop your creative thinking. Sequences exercise your memory and attention. Memory match or memories brighten up your leisure anywhere. You just need to match a pair to a hidden picture. Simple and fun!

Jigsaw puzzle. There are 60 jigsaw levels with marvelous pictures in each category. Drag parts of the picture to the right place in order to assemble the image. Use “Hint” to see the original picture. When completed levels are unlocked, you may save the images in the photo gallery of your phone. If it is hard to complete any jigsaw puzzle, press “Skip” and try to complete it later.

Hidden objects. The aim is to find hidden objects on each level. Use “Hint” and “Zoom” to find all the items as quick as possible. You may play at any level. Completed levels are unlocked for replay with various sets of hidden objects.


Memory match (memories). Find all the similar pairs of images on the playing area. Press the next level and a new set of pictures appears.

Sequence. Keep in mind the order of images appear on the screen below. Touch the screen and bottom row disappears. Then the area with pictures appears in random order. Point at images you see in the same order as they were in the beginning. For each correct sequence you gain scores.
