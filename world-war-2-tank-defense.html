World War 2 Tank Defense

World War 2 Tank Defense is tower defense RPG action arcade. You are defender of the fortress and your task is to protect the wall from enemies. Enemies are a powerful tanks designed and used in World War 2.

iOS: https://itunes.apple.com/app/id1414857863


You have to fight with tanks from German, Soviet Union, United Kingdom and USA.


Battles will be occur on desert, winter field and rugged country. To protect the fortress you have two big cannons Turret gun and Royal gun. Select one of them before battle and destroy all enemy tanks.

Also you have special abilities:

  • • Fire strike - destroy enemy tanks by big explosion.
  • • Anti-tank barrier - poison cloud that stops and paralyzes enemy tanks.
  • • Electromagnetic shield - reflects projectiles of enemies and protects the wall of the fortress.




For the defense of your base and the destruction of the enemy tanks you gain coins. You can spend the coins to improve damage and fire rate of your guns, upgrade special abilities and increase the base energy. The more upgrades you have, the more enemy tanks you will destroy.
