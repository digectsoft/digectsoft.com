Hidden Earth

Hidden Earth is an adventure shooter game with puzzle elements. Several meteorites have fallen to the Earth and brought a terrible virus. Find all the components and create a cure for the disease. Fans of hidden objects enjoy the opportunity to search for various items in an Apocalypse world.

iOS: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/hidden-earth/id1626282736

Android: https://play.google.com/apps/testing/com.expogames.hiddenearth


You play on behalf of a surviving person. The air on the planet is unfit for human use. Oxygen is crucially important in this world.


The character wears a specific outfit to help you breathe. A map is full of oxygen tanks. You can get them as a reward for destroying a guard.




The world of Hidden Earth is tricky. Items lay all over the map. Hints help to find them by flashing them. You see it on your list. Just follow navigating arrow.




The atmosphere is apocalyptical. When you collect all the necessary components, gates open, and it is possible to cross the river by the bridge and observe the first meteorite.
