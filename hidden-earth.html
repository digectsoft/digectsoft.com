Hidden Earth: Survival

Hidden Earth: Survival is an adventure survival shooter in the world of the apocalypse. Several meteorites have fallen to the Earth and brought a terrible virus. Build your base and create a cure for the disease.

iOS: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/hidden-earth/id1626282736

Android: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.expogames.hiddenearth


You play as a survivor. The air on the planet is unfit for human use. Oxygen is crucially important in this world.


The character has a special suit for breathing. Destroy a guard to get an oxygen tank and refill your supplies.




Wild animals wander in search of food. Elephants walk quietly among the abandoned streets.




The most dangerous are tigers, leopards and crocodiles. Be careful, they can attack you and damage the suit.




Find all the necessary items for the modules, assemble them and bring the base into operation.
