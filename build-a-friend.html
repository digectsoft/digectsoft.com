Build A Friend

The game world consists of a runner and a management adventure. Robots are our friends now!

Steam: https://store.steampowered.com/app/3009910

iOS: https://apps.apple.com/app/id6467549551

Android: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.expogames.buildafriend


In a runner, you create a robot following the sample on each level.

Each time you should choose the right door to win at the end of a level.


On your way, you have such obstacles as a breaker, a laser, a press, and a fire gun.


The money you collect on a runner level may be spent on your factory improvement.


Bright surroundings and 3D sound effects merge you into a wonderful world of fun and entertainment.

Try to become a part of this marvelous universe, and you will be absolutely fascinated!
